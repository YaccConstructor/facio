Facio

IelrTables<'Nonterminal, 'Terminal, 'Lookahead>

Computed tables used in the IELR(1) algorithm.

Record Fields

Record FieldDescription
always_follows
Signature: Map<unit,unit>

Records GOTO follow tokens that do not depend on the kernel item lookahead sets of predecessor states. That is, this table records GOTO follows that will never change no matter how IELR(1) may split the LALR(1) parse states.
follow_kernel_items
Signature: Map<unit,unit>

Records dependencies of GOTO follow sets on kernel item lookahead sets.
predecessors
Signature: Map<unit,unit>

Records transition predecessor relations between LALR(1) states.
